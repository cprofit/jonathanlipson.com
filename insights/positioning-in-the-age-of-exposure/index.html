Positioning in the Age of Exposure

3-minute read

What exposure changes

  • It compresses time. A slip can hit retailers and forums before your team meets about it.
  • It collapses context. Screenshots travel without tone so intent gets lost.
  • It moves price pressure to the front. Perceived value either strengthens margin or punishes it.

How to respond

  • Clarify your promise in one line. Everyone on the team should know it.
  • Audit the customer journey quarterly. Remove friction that contradicts your promise.
  • Publish positions early. Do not let forums define you.
  • Prepare a recovery script. Acknowledge, correct and show receipts.

Bottom line

Positioning is not what you print, it is the way your operation behaves under light. Control the story with consistent behavior and your margin will thank you.
